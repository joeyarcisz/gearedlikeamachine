**Subject line:** The DP is responsible for audio. Yes, really.

**Body:**

Bad sound cannot be fixed in post — and most of what the sound mixer is fighting is controlled by the DP, not the sound department. The generator you parked wrong, the ballast you chose without asking, the frame line you called without giving the boom room to work. New post on the GLM blog breaks down every decision point where the DP either protects the audio or creates a problem the entire post schedule pays for. There is also an interactive tool that lets you build a simulated noise floor from your lighting package and see when a location tips from recordable to unusable. Worth five minutes before your next tech scout.
