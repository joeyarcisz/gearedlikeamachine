## LinkedIn

New post on the GLM blog: The Grip Truck Is a Lighting Instrument.

Most DPs discuss lighting in additive terms: which fixture, which temperature, which intensity. The subtractive side, flags, negative fill, diffusion decisions, is where images get their shape and depth. These are grip department decisions, and they are just as consequential as anything in the lighting package.

The post walks through the complete framework: negative fill mechanics, the four functions of a flag, the diffusion ladder from open face to a 12x12 silk, and why the best DPs brief the key grip and gaffer at the same time. There is also an interactive tool that demonstrates each concept live.
