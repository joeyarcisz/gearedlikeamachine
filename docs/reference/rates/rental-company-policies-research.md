# 1. LENSRENTALS (lensrentals.com)

**Headquarters:** Memphis, TN
**Scale:** 400,000+ copies of 6,000+ items - the largest online rental house in the US

## Pricing Structure

- **Rental tiers:** 1-day through 90-day rentals available; price auto-calculates based on selected dates
- **Daily rate model:** The base rental period is effectively 4 days (minimum shipped rental). 1-day rates exist but are only slightly lower than 3-4 day rates, meaning shorter rentals have minimal price differentiation
- **Weekly vs daily:** The 7-day rate is significantly cheaper than 7x the daily rate. Exact multiplier varies by item but follows declining per-day cost as duration increases
- **Monthly rates:** Available; pricing decreases per-day as rental length increases up to 90 days
- **No prep/checkout fees:** Pricing is all-inclusive with shipping
- **Weekend specials:** Not explicitly offered as a named program; you simply pick your dates

## Rental Periods & Policies

- **Minimum rental:** No strict minimum for local; **4-day minimum effective** for shipped orders due to transit time
- **How days are counted:** Rental period begins at close of business (5 PM) on the day of first delivery attempt or scheduled start date, whichever is later. Runs in 24-hour increments. Must be returned to shipping carrier by end of rental period
  - Example: 7-day rental starting Tuesday begins at 5 PM Tuesday, must ship back by 5 PM the following Tuesday
- **Ship day does NOT count** as a rental day; arrival day starts the clock
- **Late return policy:**
  - On 4-day rentals: late fee = **25% of rental fee per day**, starting Day 1 overdue
  - On 7+ day rentals: late fee = 25% of rental fee per day, starting **Day 2 overdue** (1-day grace)
  - If late return forces Lensrentals to overnight-ship gear to a waiting customer, the actual overnight shipping cost is additionally charged
- **Cancellation policy:**
  - Before order leaves warehouse: **No fee, full refund**
  - After order ships/in transit: Refund minus the lesser of **$100 per box** or the **1-day rental rate**
- **Extension policy:**
  - Can extend online before rental ends
  - Extensions requested on or before ship-back date: **10% discount** off standard rates
  - Extensions requested after order is overdue: charged at **full rate** (no discount)
  - Note: Extending is always more expensive than booking a longer period upfront (e.g., 7-day rental + 7-day extension costs more than a single 14-day rental)

## Insurance & Liability

### LensCap (Standard Protection)
- **Cost:** ~**15% of rental cost** added per item
- **Covers:** Accidental damage to equipment (drops, spills - but NOT submersion/major water)
- **Requirement:** Equipment must be returnable (even if damaged)
- **Deductible:** Liability limited to **lesser of repair cost OR 10% of replacement value**
- **Does NOT cover:** Theft, total loss, water/sand damage (considered negligence), loss in transit, confiscation by airline/TSA

### LensCap+ (Enhanced Protection)
- **Cost:** ~**25% of rental cost** added per item
- **Covers:** Everything in standard PLUS theft, fire, lightning, windstorms, earthquakes, drop from aircraft, and situations where equipment cannot be returned
- **Deductible:** Same as standard - lesser of repair cost or **10% of replacement value**
- **Liability cap:** $100,000 per incident. Amounts above $100,000 are **100% renter's liability**
- **Theft claims:** Require documentation of loss and allow for investigation before liability is limited
- **Does NOT cover:** Water/sand damage (negligence), loss in transit, TSA confiscation

### Third-Party Insurance
- Lensrentals **accepts certificates of insurance** from third-party production insurance
- COI must cover rented production equipment for at least the replacement value
- COI must name **Lensrentals as additional loss payee**
- For **high-value items** (varies, but typically $10K+), a COI or LensCap+ purchase is **required**
- Purchasing LensCap+ can **waive the COI requirement** in most cases
- If renter has their own business insurance, Lensrentals expects repair bills settled promptly; they will NOT work directly with renter's insurance company

### Security Deposits
- **99.9% of orders require NO deposit**
- Deposit may be requested when order verification information is insufficient
- Deposit may be requested for very high-value items without insurance coverage

## Shipping & Logistics

- **Carriers:** Default is **FedEx**; UPS available at checkout but significantly more expensive
- **Return shipping:** **Included in rental price** - prepaid return label in box
- **Packaging:** Gear arrives in custom foam/padded shipping boxes, close box back up and reapply return label
- **Arrival timing:** Ships 1-3 days before scheduled arrival date; rental starts on arrival day
- **Return process:** Slap return label over original label, drop at FedEx (or UPS if selected). Get a receipt
- **Local pickup:** Available at Memphis, TN headquarters
- **Free shipping:** Available via **Lensrentals HD membership**

### Lensrentals HD Membership
- **Cost:** $99/year + tax
- **Benefits:**
  - Free standard shipping (FedEx or UPS) on all orders for 1 year
  - 50% off overnight shipping
  - Pays for itself in 3-4 rentals

## Payment & Credit

- **Payment methods:** Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, Discover - any major credit or debit card (NO prepaid cards)
- **When charged:** Credit card charged on the **day order ships** (1-3 days before arrival)
- **No deposit** for vast majority of orders
- **Net terms/commercial accounts:** Not publicly documented; likely available for large production companies by contacting sales

## Customer Communication

- Order confirmation at booking
- Shipping notification with tracking when order ships
- Prepaid return label included in box
- Extension reminders/options via account dashboard
- Support via help portal and phone

## Unique Programs

### Keeper Program (Buy Used Rental Gear)
- **Keeper Test Drive:** Rent eligible gear, get a purchase price based on the copy's age. Up to **7 days of rental fees** applied toward purchase price
- **Direct Purchase:** Buy button on product pages to view stock and pricing; items tested and rated for cosmetics, performance, glass quality
- **Warranty:** Items valued at **$1,000+ get a 90-day limited warranty** included
- **Returns:** 30-day return window after delivery

### Lensrentals HD Membership
- See Shipping section above

# 2. BORROWLENSES (borrowlenses.com) - NOW PART OF LENSRENTALS

**Status:** Acquired by Lensrentals March 2024. Brand discontinued. Policies below are historical/legacy.

## Pricing Structure (Historical)

- **Rental periods offered:** 3, 7, 10, 14 days and custom durations
- **3-day minimum** was standard for shorter rentals
- **Weekly rates:** Discounted vs daily; exact multipliers varied by item

## Rental Periods & Policies (Historical)

- **Cancellation policy:**
  - Rentals of 1 week or longer: Cancel before the 7-day shipping window = **no fee**
  - Cancel within 7-day shipping window: charged a **3-day rental rate**
  - 3-day rental cancellation: **$25 fee**
- **Late fees:** 25% of the 4-day rate per item per day late (similar to current Lensrentals)

## Insurance & Liability (Historical)

- **Insurance cost:** Added at checkout (pricing varied by item)
- **Coverage:** Accidental/unintentional damage only
- **Deductible:** **12% of replacement value** of item in similar condition
- **NOT covered:** Lost or stolen items
- **Third-party insurance:** Suggested renters use homeowner's, renter's, or photographer's insurance for theft/loss
- **Deposits:** Not charged

## Shipping & Logistics (Historical)

- Shipped from San Carlos, CA (now closed)
- FedEx was primary carrier
- Prepaid return labels included
- Local pickup was available at San Carlos location (discontinued March 2024)

## Unique Programs (Historical)

- **Loyalty/rewards:** Not documented in detail; brand is now defunct
- **Transition:** BorrowLenses gift cards and rental credits were transferred to Lensrentals accounts

# 3. ADORAMA RENTALS (adoramarentals.com)

**Headquarters:** Brooklyn, NY (370 19th Street, between 7th-8th Ave)
**Secondary Location:** Manhattan (42 W. 18th Street, Chelsea - pickup/drop-off only)
**Focus:** NYC-centric, walk-in rental house with mail-order capability

## Pricing Structure

- **Day Rate:** One 24-hour period
- **Day Bonus Rate:** Pick up **after 4:30 PM**, return by **9:30 AM two days later** = charged the 1-day rate (essentially a free overnight)
- **Week Rate:** Seven consecutive 24-hour periods (specific multiplier not publicly listed; quote-based)
- **3-Day Weekend Rate:** Pick up **Thursday after 4:30 PM** or anytime Friday, return **Monday by 9:30 AM** = charged the **1-day rate**
- **No publicly listed prices:** Must request via Quick Quote or call 212-627-8487
- **No documented prep/checkout fees**

## Rental Periods & Policies

- **Minimum rental (in-store):** 1 day (24 hours)
- **Minimum rental (mail order):** **1 week** - shipped overnight, renter pays both-way shipping
- **How days are counted (mail order):** Rental starts when you receive the order, ends when Adorama receives it back
- **Return deadline:** Equipment due back by **9:30 AM on return date** (except "24 Hour Rentals")
- **Late fees:** Full day's rental charged for equipment not returned by 9:30 AM of due date. Continued daily rate accrual until all items returned
- **Cancellation policy:**
  - Cancel **24+ hours** before scheduled pickup: **No fee**
  - No-show or cancel within 24 hours: **1-day rental fee** charged
- **Prep time:**
  - New customers: **1 business day** for verification
  - Repeat customers: **Minimum 2 hours notice**
  - Large orders: Additional prep time required

## Insurance & Liability

### Short-Term Insurance (via Film Emporium)
- **Coverage:** Up to **$50,000** in equipment value
- **Duration:** Up to **4 weeks** rental
- **Sold by:** Film Emporium through Adorama Rental Co
- **Non-refundable** transaction
- **Requirement:** Credit card and valid US photo ID must be present by cardholder at pickup
- **Not available** to international customers

### Insurance Requirements by Value
- **Replacement cost up to $15K:** Renter can place security deposit on credit card **OR** buy insurance
- **Replacement cost over $15K:** Insurance is **REQUIRED** - must provide COI with Adorama Rental Co as loss payee
- **With COI:** Only the deductible amount (or rental fee, whichever is higher) is held on credit card

### Security Deposit
- **Required for all rentals:** Credit card authorized for the **full replacement value** of equipment
- Authorization (hold) released within **72 hours** of return, minus rental fees
- Rental fee itself is charged **when equipment is returned** (not at booking)

### Damage/Loss
- If equipment is lost, stolen, destroyed, or damaged beyond repair: renter pays **full replacement value**
- Damage assessment provided after return inspection
- Renter indemnifies and holds Adorama harmless against any claims from equipment use

## Shipping & Logistics

- **Mail order:** Available inside the US only, minimum 1-week rental
- **Carrier:** **UPS** (return labels included)
- **Shipping cost:** Renter pays **both ways**
- **Packaging:** Professional packaging with return label included
- **Local pickup:** Available at both locations

### Location Hours
**Brooklyn (370 19th Street) - Full service:**
- Monday-Thursday: 9 AM - 5 PM
- Friday: 9 AM - 5 PM
- Saturday-Sunday: Closed

**Manhattan (42 W. 18th Street) - Pickup/drop-off only:**
- Monday-Thursday: 10 AM - 5 PM
- Friday: 10 AM - 3 PM
- Saturday-Sunday: Closed
- Note: Cinema camera packages requiring full checkout NOT supported here

**Curbside service:** Available at both locations - call 212-627-8487, option #2

## Payment & Credit

- **Payment methods:** Major non-debit credit cards (Visa, MC, AmEx) - must be non-debit, non-prepaid
- **ID required:** Valid US photo ID
- **Age requirement:** Must be 18+
- **When charged:** Authorization hold at pickup for full replacement value; rental fee charged **at return**
- **Credit line requirement:** Must have sufficient credit line for full replacement value
- **Net terms/commercial:** Not publicly documented

## Customer Communication

- Quote-based ordering (Quick Quote form or phone)
- Order confirmation upon booking
- Not heavily documented for automated communications

## Unique Aspects

- **NYC-focused:** Ideal for NYC production companies needing same-day equipment
- **Day Bonus Rate:** Essentially a free overnight if you pick up after 4:30 PM
- **3-Day Weekend Rate:** Huge value - Thursday evening through Monday morning for 1-day rate
- **Curbside service:** Don't even need to leave your vehicle
- **Full-service Brooklyn location** with cinema department

# 4. LENSPROTOGO (lensprotogo.com)

**Headquarters:** 130B Baker Ave Extension, Concord, MA 01742
**Hours:** Monday-Friday 9 AM - 6 PM, closed weekends
**Phone:** 877-578-4777

## Pricing Structure

- **Pricing includes shipping:** Up to 2-day round-trip shipping built into rental price
- **Minimum rental (shipped):** **4 days** - all shipped orders subject to 4-day minimum rate regardless of actual use
- **Minimum rental (pickup):** **3 days**
- **Daily/weekly/monthly rates:** Vary by item; calculated at checkout
- **No publicly listed rate formulas** - prices shown per item on product pages
- **No documented prep/checkout fees**

## Rental Periods & Policies

- **How days are counted:** Rental starts the **day gear arrives** (generally afternoon) and ends the **day you ship gear back**
- **Return deadline (shipped):** Must ship back before **3 PM** on last day of rental; items returned after are subject to an additional rental day
- **Return deadline (pickup):** Due by **4 PM** on last day; items after 4 PM subject to additional rental day
- **Pickup availability:** Pick-up orders ready by **11 AM** on start day; early pickup available **9 AM - 11 AM** if gear is in stock the day prior
- **Late return fees:** **25% of the 4-day rate** per item per day late, PLUS regular extension cost
  - Example: If 4-day rate on a lens is $100, late fee = $25/day + extension rate
- **Cancellation policy:**
  - Cancel any time **before order ships:** No fee
  - Cannot cancel after shipping; once shipped, the **4-day minimum fee applies**
- **Extension policy:**
  - Extend online via "My Account" > "Currently Renting" > "Extend your rental"
  - Must request before **5 PM EST** on last day of rental
  - Some items may not be eligible for extension due to limited quantity/scheduling

## Insurance & Liability

### Protection Plan (Standard)
- **Cost:** Flat fee per rental (varies by item); **same price regardless of rental duration**
- **Covers:** Repair or replacement if equipment is **damaged**
- **Deductible:** **10% of replacement value**
  - Example: $1,000 lens damaged requiring $300 repair - you pay $100 (10% of $1K), they pay $200
- **Does NOT cover:** Theft, loss, water/sand/particulate damage, negligence

### Protection Plan Plus (Enhanced)
- **Covers:** Everything in standard PLUS theft, loss, and situations where equipment cannot be returned
- **Deductible:** **10% of replacement value** up to **$25,000 per item**
- **Liability cap:** **$100,000 per incident** - losses over $100K are 100% renter's responsibility
- **Theft claims:** Must provide police report; investigation may be required

### Third-Party Insurance
- Renter may provide **certificate of insurance** to avoid credit card authorization hold
- If no protection plan purchased: renter is **100% liable** for repair or replacement costs

### Security Deposits / Authorization Holds
- Most rentals do **NOT** require additional documents or deposits
- For high-value rentals: credit card authorization hold may be placed for equipment value
- Hold released upon satisfactory return

## Shipping & Logistics

- **Included shipping:** Up to **2-day round-trip FedEx** shipping included in price
- **Primary carrier:** **FedEx** (preferred)
- **Packaging:** **Pelican hard cases** inside cardboard shipping boxes - gear stays protected
- **Return process:** Pack gear back in Pelican case, put in cardboard box, reseal, attach prepaid return label
- **Return location:** Must return to a **staffed FedEx location** where you can get a receipt - drop boxes NOT recommended (pickup schedules unreliable, could cause delays/fees)
- **Local pickup:** Available at Concord, MA headquarters and at **Hunt's Photo & Video locations** (4,000+ locations via FedEx/Hunt's partnership)
  - Hunt's locations: Order shipped to Hunt's store, notified via email when ready (generally after 4 PM)
- **Weekend orders:** Submitted over weekend, processed Monday

## Payment & Credit

- **Payment methods:** Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, Discover (major credit and debit cards)
- **When charged:**
  - Orders placed within 1 week of arrival: charged **shortly after submission**
  - Longer-term reservations: charged **1 week before rental start date**
  - All orders: charged **2 business days prior to shipment**
- **No deposit** for most rentals
- **Authorization holds** possible for high-value items

## Customer Communication

- Order confirmation at booking
- Shipping notification with tracking
- Hunt's Photo location pickup notification via email
- Extension options available online in account dashboard
- Support via phone (877-578-4777) and email

## Unique Programs

### Rental Rewards Loyalty Program
- **How it works:** Complete **7 orders**, earn credit toward a **free rental on order #8**
- **Credit amount:** Based on the **average rental dollars spent** (after discounts, shipping, taxes) across the 7 qualifying orders
- **Reward timing:** Punches awarded once gear is returned and order finalized (2-5 business days after gear received)
- **Expiration:** **12 months** from date of issue to use reward credit
- **Terms:** https://www.lensprotogo.com/rental-rewards-terms-conditions/

### Pelican Case Packaging
- All shipments arrive in hard-shell Pelican cases - a notable quality differentiator over cardboard-only competitors

# KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR GLM RENTAL POLICIES

## Industry Standards to Adopt
1. **4-day minimum for shipped rentals** is universal
2. **Rental starts on delivery day**, not ship day
3. **Prepaid return labels** are expected
4. **Insurance/protection plans** are standard - offer basic (damage) and enhanced (theft/loss)
5. **10% of replacement value** is the standard deductible
6. **No deposit for most rentals** (Adorama is the exception, but they're walk-in focused)
7. **Cancel before ship = free** is universal
8. **Late fees of 25% of rental rate per day** is common
9. **Extensions should be available online** before rental ends

## Differentiators to Consider
1. **Weekend specials** (Adorama's 3-day weekend = 1 day rate is brilliant for local rentals)
2. **Pelican case packaging** (LensProToGo's approach signals premium quality)
3. **Loyalty program** (LensProToGo's 7-orders-for-1-free is simple and effective)
4. **Buy-the-gear option** (Lensrentals' Keeper program - not applicable for small inventory but the concept of rental credit toward purchase is interesting)
5. **Membership programs** for frequent renters (Lensrentals HD at $99/yr for free shipping)
6. **Day Bonus Rate** concept (Adorama) - pick up evening, return next morning = 1 day

## GLM-Specific Considerations
- As a boutique/local Dallas operation, the Adorama model (walk-in focused, quote-based, weekend specials) is most relevant
- For shipped rentals, the Lensrentals/LensProToGo model (all-inclusive pricing, prepaid returns) is the standard to match
- Insurance: Partner with a provider like Film Emporium or build a simple damage waiver into pricing
- Since GLM is small-scale: manual booking confirmation (like current plan) is fine, but clearly document policies upfront
