## Sources Used
- lensrentals.com (primary reference)

## Research Method
5 parallel agents researched all 156 items across categories: Audio, Camera/Drone, Lenses, Lighting, Support/Grip/Power/Monitoring. Rates were cross-referenced across multiple rental houses and applied as owner-operator daily rates.
