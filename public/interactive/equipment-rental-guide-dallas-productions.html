Booking timeline

DFW market booking windows. Miss these and you are scrambling.

14+ DAYS BEFORE SHOOT
Book premium prime sets
Cookes, Leicas, and ZEISS Supremes book out fast on weekend shoots. National house inventory goes first. Reserve or lose your lens choice.
10+ DAYS BEFORE SHOOT
Lock ARRI and VENICE body reservations
Cinema bodies have a deeper pool than primes but still book out on Thursdays through Mondays. Your camera determines your entire support package.
7 DAYS BEFORE SHOOT
Confirm insurance certificate
The rental house will not release gear without it. A DICE or single-production policy takes 24-48 hours to issue. Do not wait until day-before.
5 DAYS BEFORE SHOOT
Order shipped gear with a buffer day
National houses ship 1-day to Dallas. Order for delivery 2 days before the shoot. One day to QC, one day of cushion if anything needs replacement.
2 DAYS BEFORE SHOOT
Full gear QC on arrival
Check everything. Lenses for barrel smoothness and aperture clicks. Camera body sensor and menu functions. Lights for output consistency. Any issue needs time to resolve.
DAY OF SHOOT
Local grip and expendables pickup only
If anything is missing from your primary package, source locally. This is the only same-day category. Camera, lens, and lighting must already be at the location.
