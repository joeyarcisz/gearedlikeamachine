Tool 04 / 05

The Prep Timeline

Five weeks to camera day

Ask for five weeks on small-to-medium budget projects. Every week has a distinct focus. Let one bleed into the next and you arrive underprepared.

The more you understand your location before you shoot, the easier and more efficient you will be on set. A good plan saves time. Time is money. Prep is not overhead.
