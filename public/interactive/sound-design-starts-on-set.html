02 — Room Tone Protocol

30 seconds. Non-negotiable.

Room tone is the acoustic signature of the space captured in exact shooting conditions. Record it after every setup, every new location, every time the environment changes significantly.

04 — Wild Lines vs ADR

The insurance policy you collect before wrap.

Select a rescue method for each compromised take. The cost and quality difference between recording on the day versus returning to a studio months later is almost never worth it.
